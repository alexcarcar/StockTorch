### Price Action

- Study of how **price moves** on a chart
- Without relying on **indicators**

| **Candlestick patterns** | **Market Structure**          | **Market Concepts**             |
|--------------------------|-------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| - Engulfing              | - Higher highs/lows           | - Breakouts and false breakouts |
| - Doji                   | - Trends/Ranges               | - Buyer vs. seller pressure     |
| - Momentum Candles       | - Support & Resistance Levels | - Price Action vs. Indicators   |

### Price Action vs. Indicators

| **Price Action**                  | **Indicators**                           |
|-----------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Uses raw price movement           | Uses calculated values (RSI, MACD, etc.) |
| Real‑time, no lag                 | Often lag behind price                   |
| Focus on structure & candlesticks | Focus on mathematical signals            |
| More flexible and interpretive    | More rule‑based and mechanical           |
| Helps read market psychology      | Helps quantify trends and momentum       |

### Candlestick Patterns

1. Bullish Engulfing

- First candle: small bearish (red)
- Second candle: large bullish (green) that fully engulfs the previous body

2. Doji

- Open and close are nearly identical
- Shows market indecision

3. Momentum Candle

- Large candle body
- Strong directional move (in this example, bullish)
